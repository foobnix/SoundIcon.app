Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Sound Icon is built with your privacy in mind. We believe that your data is your own. Our application operates entirely locally on your macOS device.

  • We do not collect personal information.
  • We do not track your usage, audio device choices, or media playback habits.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or crash reporting tools that phone home.

2. Local Data Storage

Any settings or preferences you configure within Sound Icon (such as your starred audio devices or chosen custom icons) are stored locally on your Mac. This data never leaves your device.

3. Network Access

Sound Icon does not require an active internet connection to function and does not transmit any data over the internet.

4. Changes to This Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy in the future, we will update the Privacy Policy modification date at the top of this page. However, our commitment to a non-tracking, local-first application will remain our priority.
